How the app works · Clients

• Create a profile with your basic info and preferred payment methods (Apple Pay, Cash App, PayPal, debit card note).
• Request a pickup with your address, notes (delicates, stains, repairs), and timing.
• A washer accepts your load, picks it up, washes/dries/sews as agreed, and returns it.
• You pay the washer directly using the payment method you both agree on—Laundry Bubbles does not hold or move money.


How the app works · Washers

• Create a profile with your skills (wash, dry, fold, sew), service area, and payment handles.
• Set your availability and watch incoming requests in your dashboard.
• Accept jobs you want, chat with clients (later), pick up, complete, and return loads.
• Get paid directly by clients via Apple Pay, Cash App, PayPal, or card—no platform payout delays.
